Paris - The City of Lights


Paris, the capital of France, is one of the most beautiful and romantic cities in the world. Known for its stunning architecture, rich history, and world-class cuisine, Paris attracts millions of visitors every year.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – The most iconic landmark of Paris, offering breathtaking views of the city.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the famous Mona Lisa and other incredible artworks.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A stunning example of French Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées – A lively avenue filled with luxury shops, cafes, and theaters.
  5. Seine River Cruise – A scenic way to explore Paris from the water.

Must-Try Foods

  • French Croissant – A buttery and flaky pastry, perfect for breakfast.
  • Ratatouille – A traditional vegetable dish packed with flavors.
  • Escargots de Bourgogne – Snails cooked in garlic butter, a classic French delicacy.
  • Macarons – Colorful and delicious almond-based cookies.
  • Coq au Vin – A hearty dish made with chicken slow-cooked in red wine.
